Summary

Video games have always been a passion of mine. But one goal I set out to achieve was to build and grow a community that is safe and inclusive through writing. My attendance at Indiana University Bloomington assisted in learning to collaborate and participate with people from many cultural backgrounds. As part of a program called GROUPS, we worked together to solve problems and learned to articulate myself in a respectful manner. This is shown in my Medium blog.


While managing my Medium blog, I’ve authored various articles about video games. My extensive knowledge of the Yakuza franchise led to two reviews that people liked on both Medium as well as YouTube.
